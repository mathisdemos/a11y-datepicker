a11y-datepicker

This is a datepicker. You can use your keyboard to type in a date. You can also use the datepicker modal. Press Shift and Arrow Down to open the modal or to move focus there. Press Arrow Right to move one day back. Press Arrow Left to move one day ahead. Press Arrow Up to move one week back. Press Arrow Down to move one week ahead. Press Space to select a date. Press Escape to close the modal.
